Call For Papers



Scope

The First International Conference on Signal Processing on Information, Network and Computer (ICSINC) focuses on the key technologies and challenges of signal processing on information theory, network application, computer theory and application and so on.

The research areas are convergence by the similarity with both algorithms and applications. The current state and trend of signal processing will be exchanged in this conference. This conference solicits both original research and tutorial articles that investigate the key technologies in various scenarios.


Submissions

Only PDF files will be accepted for the review process and all submissions must be done through EasyChair. Papers should be written in English with a maximum paper length of eight (8) printed pages including figures without incurring additional page charges. Submissions will be reviewed by at least 3 reviewers and judged on originality, correctness, clarity and relevance. Submitted papers must be original work, and may not be under consideration for another conference or journal. Submission of the paper implies agreement of the author(s) to attend the conference and present the paper if accepted.

The paper submissions must follow the formatting guidelines (see Author's kit section).


Publication

All accepted papers will be published by Taylor & Francis Group and be submitted for EI indexing.
